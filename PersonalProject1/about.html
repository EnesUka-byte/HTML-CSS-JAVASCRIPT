About Gift Card Shop

Welcome to Gift Card Shop! We are passionate about making gifting easy and fun. Our mission is to offer a wide variety of gift cards from the most popular brands, ensuring that everyone finds something perfect for any occasion.

Our team carefully selects and curates gift cards for entertainment, gaming, shopping, and mobile use, delivering a seamless online shopping experience for our customers.

Our Story

Founded in 2025 as a school project, Gift Card Shop has quickly grown into a demo platform showcasing modern e-commerce design. Although prices and checkout are placeholders, we aim to provide a realistic shopping experience and a foundation for future development.
